Hands Off My Face!

A simple tool to keep you from touching your face in times of the Coronavirus.

Works best (and possibly only) in Google Chrome Desktop (not Mobile).

Based on PoseNet - runs with either a single-pose or multi-pose detection algorithm. The single person pose detector is faster and more accurate but requires only one subject present in the image.

The output stride and input resolution have the largest effects on accuracy/speed. A higher output stride results in lower accuracy but higher speed. A higher image scale factor results in higher accuracy but lower speed.
